Who We Are

MAYPRI was established by Mayur Mathur and Priya Mathur in 2011 as a food & beverage franchise venture. It holds business interests in the hospitality and healthcare sectors through its group companies Maypri Foods Pvt Ltd & Maypri Healthcare Pvt Ltd.

MAYUR MATHUR

Director

A passionate and committed individual, has a Bachelors of Engineering degree in Electronics & Telcommunication from MIT, Pune and a diploma in management in Finance from SIIB Pune. Having worked for 5 years with ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd in the Underwriting and Risk Management division, in 2011 he along with Priya Mathur founded MAYPRI by venturing into the franchise business in the F&B industry.

PRIYA MATHUR

Director

She holds a Bachelors of Commerce from Maharani College, Jaipur and a diploma in management in Finance from SIIB Pune. She has worked for 2 years in ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd & Iffco Tokio General Insurance in the Corporate Operations division. She also has extensive experience as a stock market consultant. In 2011 she along with Mayur Mathur founded MAYPRI by venturing into the franchise business in the F&B industry.
