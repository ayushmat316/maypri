MR. VENDO

Maypri Foods Pvt Ltd are authorised technology partners with E-cube India Solutions Ltd, leading manufacturers of SmartVend - Automatic Food & Snack vending machines, for Noida, Greater Noida , Ghaziabad & Jaipur cities.
Such vending setups are very popular in western countries where most hotels, offices, commercial buildings and highway rest areas have this facility so that people can get quick access to snacks and drinks 24x7.
The machine is fully automatic and does not require human intervention for daily operations. It's as easy as inserting the currency/coins or using an access card and pressing the item number in the keypad. They are also hygienic because the food items are in sealed areas and can be picked up by the person itself without a shop-keeper, operator or anyone else touching it.
These are high-tech machines that have refrigerated areas as well where soft drinks can be kept refrigerated.


We offer an end-to-end vending operations business model right from installation, maintenance, stock replenishment to day-to-day operations of the vending machines.
We have two business models:

- Client-Owned-Vendor-Operated (COVO)
- Vendor-Owned-Vendor-Operated (VOVO)
- Vendor-Owned-Client-Operated (VOCO)
