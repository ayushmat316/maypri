What is Vendistro?

An initiative by Maypri Foods Pvt Ltd, offers an end-to-end solution for corporates, IT Companies, institutions looking for on-site round-the-clock availability of snacks and beverages at the convenience of their employees , through our automatic snack vending machines

Why Choose Vendistro?

Time, Convenience & Choice is of utmost importance in today's fast-moving life. Vendistro provides all these elements gracefully!
Our vending machines offer unique benefits:

  • Provide convenient, fresh availability of snacks and beverages
  • Ease of Use and take up minimal space
  • Night-time refreshments - Are ideal for offices / Companies where employees work hard during the night hours
  • Offer a good snack-on-the-go and employees who want to grab a bite
  • Quick availability of snack for an in-house corporate meeting/conference
  • Holds more than 200 products at a time
